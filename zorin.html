What is Zorin OS?

Zorin OS is a user-friendly Linux operating system that looks and feels familiar to Windows or macOS, runs fast on older hardware, and keeps your data private. It’s perfect for reviving old PCs/Macs or avoiding upgrade headaches.

Familiar layouts (Windows/macOS) Fast on older hardware Private & secure App Store + Flatpak/Snap Dual-boot friendly

Why choose Zorin OS?

  • Speed boost: Lighter than Windows; boots fast and stays snappy even on HDDs.
  • Security & privacy: Built-in Linux security model; minimal telemetry.
  • Looks how you want: Choose a layout that mimics Windows or macOS so it feels natural immediately.
  • Software your way: Zorin App Store, plus Flatpak, Snap, and apt repos.
  • Great on old hardware: Bring life back to machines that struggle with newer Windows versions.
  • Low maintenance: Simple updates; no naggy forced restarts.

Can I do what I did on Windows or Mac?

  • Web & email: Chrome/Chromium, Firefox, Brave, Thunderbird.
  • Office: LibreOffice (Word/Excel/PowerPoint compatible), OnlyOffice; Microsoft 365 / Google Workspace in the browser.
  • Media: VLC, Plex client/server, Spotify.
  • Photos & design: GIMP, Inkscape, Krita, Darktable.
  • Gaming: Steam with Proton, Lutris, retro emulators (hardware permitting).
  • Windows apps: Many run via Wine/Bottles; we test your needs and suggest native alternatives if better.

What we set up for you

  • Desktop layout matching your comfort (Windows-style or macOS-style).
  • Browsers, bookmarks, extensions, and passwords (where possible).
  • Email accounts, printers, and drivers.
  • Backup plan and restore test.
  • Plex server or media sharing (optional).
  • Dual-boot with Windows, or migrate fully — your choice.

FAQ

Will my files move over? Yes — we migrate your Documents, Pictures, and other libraries. We can also mount your old Windows/Mac drive for access.

Can I still use Microsoft Office? You can use Microsoft 365 in the browser, or install Office-compatible suites. We’ll validate your key workflows.

What about drivers? Zorin handles most hardware out of the box. We’ll install extras (Wi-Fi, graphics, printers) as needed.

Can I go back? Dual-boot keeps Windows available, or we can fully revert from your backups.
