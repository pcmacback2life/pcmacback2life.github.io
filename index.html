Example: How We Move Your Data to Your New Zorin Computer

Step 1 – Collecting Your Files

We begin by connecting a USB flash drive or external hard drive to your old PC or Mac. This becomes the “moving truck” for your digital belongings.

Step 2 – Packing Up

We carefully copy your important folders—Documents, Pictures, Music, Desktop, and anything else you choose—onto the USB drive. If your old computer has multiple user profiles, we’ll gather the files from each one so no one’s data is left behind.

Step 3 – Safe Transfer

Once everything is copied, we safely remove the USB and connect it to your new Zorin OS computer.

Step 4 – Unpacking on Zorin OS

We place your files into the right spots on Zorin—Documents, Pictures, Music—so everything is organized and familiar. If there were multiple profiles on your old computer, we’ll create matching profiles on Zorin and place each person’s files in their own account.

Step 5 – Secure Login Setup

When you receive your new Zorin PC or Mac, we provide a temporary password for each user profile. Along with it, you’ll get simple step-by-step instructions on how to change the password to something unique and personal to you.

What is a Plex Server and Why You Might Want One

Think of Plex as your own personal Netflix.
Instead of paying for streaming subscriptions to access movies and shows you already own, Plex turns your PC or Mac into a media server. This means all your movies, TV shows, music, and even home videos live in one place — and you can enjoy them on any device in your home, like a smart TV, phone, or tablet.

Why You Need It

  • One Library for Everything: Plex organizes your collection with cover art, titles, and categories, making it easy to browse just like Netflix.
  • Simple Backups: We’ll place easy-to-use icons on your Zorin desktop so you can back up your media to a Plex folder with a single click.
  • Expandable Storage: If your drive isn’t big enough, we can upgrade it or use an external USB drive.
  • Smart TV Ready: Many smart TVs (like Samsung) already include the Plex app for quick, easy viewing.

Local vs. Remote

Local Use (Included): We configure Plex to work perfectly inside your home network — anyone on your Wi-Fi can watch from TVs, phones, tablets, or laptops.

Remote Access (Optional): Stream your library away from home. Requires router configuration and testing; offered as an advanced service at $120/hr.

Licensing

To unlock all features, a Plex license (Plex Pass) is required. We can help you purchase and activate it so everything runs smoothly from day one.

✅ Bottom Line: With Plex, your Zorin computer becomes the heart of your entertainment system. We make sure your media is organized, backed up, and ready to enjoy — whether you’re watching on your computer, a smart TV, or mobile devices.
